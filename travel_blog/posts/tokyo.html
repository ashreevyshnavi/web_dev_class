A Weekend in Tokyo

By Sophia Lee Published on May 5, 2024

Tokyo, a city of neon lights and ancient temples, offers an unforgettable experience. Here’s how to enjoy a weekend in Japan’s bustling capital.

Day 1: Exploring Shibuya and Shinjuku

Start your journey at Shibuya Crossing, visit Meiji Shrine, and experience the nightlife of Shinjuku.

Day 2: Traditional Tokyo

Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, explore Akihabara for anime culture, and end with sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market.

Where to Stay

A modern hotel in Shibuya for easy access to the city’s best spots.
