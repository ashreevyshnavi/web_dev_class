A Weekend in Italy

By Emily Roberts Published on April 20, 2024

Italy, a land of rich history, stunning landscapes, and mouth-watering cuisine. Here’s how to make the most of a weekend in Italy.

Day 1: Rome’s Ancient Wonders

Begin your day at the Colosseum, then explore the Roman Forum before indulging in an authentic Italian pizza at a local trattoria.

Day 2: Venice’s Romantic Charm

Take a gondola ride through the canals, visit St. Mark’s Basilica, and enjoy fresh seafood at a canal-side restaurant.

Where to Stay

A cozy boutique hotel in the heart of Rome for easy access to landmarks.
